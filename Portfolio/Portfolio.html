Ethics Reflection

The definition of Ethics is moral principles that govern a person’s behavior or the conducting of an activity. It is important to many different job field, especially in engineering. Because the job that an engineer does is that design and build a system that is wildly used by many people. An un‐ethics action might have cause terrible incident that would cost a lot of money or people’s life.
The purpose of having code of ethics is preventing un‐ethics action accident made by an engineer. In many cases, it would be hard for engineer to identify what action would be un‐ethics, and code of ethics identify them. When faced with an ethical situation, I would go about following the code of ethics, but also taking account of is there any other alternative to solve the problem. Also I will look at if the action would be illegal or not in the current country.
There were a few ethics case discussed within the group. One of them was the article “An Amazon Echo may be the key to solving a murder case”. It was about a murder happen inside of a house that had equipped with amazon echo. The FBI thinks that amazon echo might have send some voice record related to the murder case, so that FBI wanted amazon to release the information. Amazon actually refused to release the information to the public or the FBI.
I think the 6 virtues that is most related to case is Responsibility. Other virtues isn’t really related to the case. What responsibility is accountability, trustworthiness, dependability, reliability, and recognizing a personal and moral obligation to act for the good of others. In this case, amazon decided not to release its customer private information to the public or FIB. I think that is the responsibility that amazon should take on, it should have protected its customer private information.
